How it Works

Ship a Project

Get Shipped Japanese Stationery

01

Build something awesome for your school community

Create a website or tool that helps out a club at your school or students. The project must be open source and deployable on the web. A minimum of 5 hours of work is required to be able to qualify for Japanese stationery.

02

Submit your project

Fill out the submission form with your project details and wait for us to review you project.

03

Receive your stationery

Once your project is approved, you will receive stationery based on the quality and effort put in your project.

Questions

FAQ

What is the age requirement?

Ages 13-18

Does it have to be a website?

No it doesn't have to. There are games that you can create that qualify as well. The only requirements are that it could benefit students and is deployable on the web.

Can I submit a previous project?

Submitting a project built previously isn't allowed for Scholar.

What if I am not in the US?

You can still participate! It is free, but anyone outside the US is responsible for any customs fees.

Do I have to track my hours through Hackatime?

Yes. This YSWS requires hour tracking.

How many hours do I need to qualify for stationery?

You need to have at least 5 hours on a project to be able to qualify for stationery.

Can I choose my stationery?

More details to come soon!

Can I use AI to help?

With AI, it is better if you avoid using it for creative tasks and instead use it for research and brainstorming.
